    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: June 2026  ·  Concept copy for demonstration.

    SoulFriends LLC (“SoulFriends,” “we,” “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using soulfriends.community you agree to the practices described here.

    Information we collect

    • Information you give us — name, email, and anything you share via forms, the assessment, or membership sign-up.
    • Usage information — pages visited, device and browser type, and similar analytics collected automatically.
    • Payment information — processed securely by our third-party payment provider; we do not store full card numbers.

    How we use your information

    • To deliver memberships, gatherings, and the experiences you request.
    • To send you updates, resources, and offers you've opted into.
    • To improve our content, community, and website.
    • To meet legal and security obligations.

    Sharing

    We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers (such as payment, email, and hosting platforms) who help us operate, and only as needed to provide our services.

    Your choices

    • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in each email.
    • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by emailing us.
    • Control cookies through your browser settings.

    Data security

    We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is fully secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

    Contact

    Questions about this policy? Email hello@soulfriends.community.
