    Questions

    Before you join.

    Do I need any breathwork experience?

    None at all. Every session is fully guided, and ThetaBreath is gentle and accessible — you'll be supported from your very first breath.

    What if I can't make the live call?

    Every gathering is recorded and added to your practice library, so you can return to it whenever you need to land.

    Who is in the room?

    High-capacity women — founders, leaders, creators, healers — who are done with surface networking and ready for depth.

    Can I upgrade to a retreat later?

    Yes. Members receive priority access and pricing on Summer Camp, immersives, and FORGE.
