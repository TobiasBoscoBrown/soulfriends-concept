    Our Story

    It began with eleven women.

    Two days of breathwork and wild conversation. One question that wouldn't let go.

    The Origin

    One anchoring question.

    “What are we each truly here to do — and who do we need around us to become her?”

    SoulFriends was never meant to be just another networking space. From those first moments, something rare emerged: a space where women could exhale, soften, and be seen not for what they've achieved, but for who they truly are.

    We built soul-deep connections that transcended status, bank accounts, and bios — connections that expand creative vision, that accelerate aligned wealth, that awaken a deeper wisdom. And we knew: this is the kind of space we want for the next 40+ years.
